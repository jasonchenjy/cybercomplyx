Professional Compliance Consulting Team

Team Background

We are a compliance consulting team with rich international experience. Our core members come from KPMG, Uber, Meta, and Salesforce (the world's leading CRM software company), with over 9 years of industry experience. The team has provided compliance consulting services to more than 20 leading Silicon Valley tech companies, covering software, AI, healthcare, cryptocurrency, and fintech sectors.

Project Experience

Our team leader previously led Uber's IPO readiness project, building SOX and information security control systems to successfully meet pre-IPO compliance requirements. At Salesforce, we managed the company's global compliance reporting system (SOC, ISO, HIPAA, PCI), collaborated with overseas teams to complete international standard projects such as ISMAP (Japan) and IRAP (Australia), while participating in multiple M&A security assessments.

Service Philosophy

We combine traditional compliance consulting methods with modern technology, leveraging AI tools and automation platforms to achieve more efficient risk assessment and control implementation. During project execution, we quickly identify risk points based on the enterprise's current status, develop practical remediation paths, and help clients pass international audits and obtain authoritative certifications within limited budgets.

Service Achievements

We currently focus on supporting Chinese companies' global expansion, having successfully helped multiple healthcare, AI, SaaS, and Web3 companies obtain SOC, HIPAA, PCI, and other compliance reports, enabling their smooth entry into North American and other international markets.
